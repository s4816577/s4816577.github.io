TaiChiAR

Introduction

    In real scenario, learning Tai-Chi Chuan (TCC) is not easy. Students often rotate their heads or bodies because of the need for movements, and their view will also be blocked by other students so they may not be able to see the movements of coaches clearly. Moreover, students may have different progress when learning, and the coach does not have time to care about everyone.
    Thus, we construct a TCC AR learning system by Hololens. By wearing HoloLens, staudents can see many virtual coaches surrounding him/her. Those virtual coaches will present different perspectives, playback speed, height and learning progress according to the needs of students.

Interaction

    Hands-free control is very important in TCC training since students will need to use their hands to express the movements of grasping, releasing, and lifting. Therefore, we have designed different kinds of interactive methods, including button control, voice control and gesture control, so that students can control our AR learning system more flexibly.
